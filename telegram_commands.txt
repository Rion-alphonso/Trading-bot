XAUUSD Trading Bot - Telegram Remote Control Commands

--- 📊 Insights & Monitoring ---
/status   - Check the bot's current state (RUNNING/STOPPED), account balance, equity, martingale level, and next trade direction.
/report   - Get a daily performance summary including total trades taken, net PnL, and win rate.
/history  - View the results of your last 5 closed trades.
/open     - Inspect the currently open position (if any), including the ticket number, entry price, and level.
/chart    - Generates a live equity curve graph from your account history and sends it as an image.
/logs     - Fetch the last 15 lines of the system logs to debug any issues directly from your phone.

--- ⚙️ Execution Controls ---
/start    - Start the trading engine if it was stopped. (Same as /resume)
/resume   - Resume the trading engine and start looking for entries.
/pause    - Stop the trading engine from opening any *new* trades. (Existing trades will still be managed).
/closeall - EMERGENCY PANIC BUTTON. Instantly sends a signal to close all open positions at current market price and halts the bot.
/reset    - Manually reset your Martingale recovery streak back to Level 1.

--- 🔧 Configuration Overrides ---
/setrisk <%>           - Dynamically override the Level 1 risk percentage without restarting the bot. (Usage: /setrisk 2.0)
/direction <buy|sell>  - Force the engine to take a specific direction for its next trade, overriding the EMA indicator. (Usage: /direction buy)
/session <on|off>      - Toggle whether the bot should strictly obey the 10AM/8PM trading hours. Set to 'off' to trade 24/7. (Usage: /session off)
/config                - View all of your currently active overrides (Risk, Session, Direction).

--- 🩺 System Diagnostics ---
/health   - Check the VPS / Host machine's CPU usage, RAM usage, and SQLite database size.
